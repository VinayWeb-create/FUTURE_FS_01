Placement Prep Platform

A comprehensive full-stack platform built to help students prepare for campus placements through structured practice, company-specific resources, and AI-assisted learning.

Problem Statement

Most students prepare for placements using scattered resources such as PDFs, random YouTube videos, and multiple websites.

This lack of structure makes preparation inefficient, difficult to track, and mentally overwhelming during interviews—leading to suboptimal performance even for capable students.

Solution Overview

The Placement Preparation Web App centralizes all placement-related preparation into a single, structured platform.

It guides students through aptitude, technical, and interview preparation while tracking progress, highlighting weak areas, and providing targeted practice recommendations.

Key Features

  • CRT practice modules covering Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, and Verbal Ability
  • Technical MCQs across C, Java, Python, DBMS, Operating Systems, and Computer Networks
  • Comprehensive HR & technical interview question bank
  • Company-specific preparation resources (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, etc.)
  • Timed quizzes with automatic scoring and detailed analytics
  • Progress dashboard with performance insights and weak area identification
  • AI-based doubt clarification and concept explanation

System Architecture

The frontend provides a clean, responsive interface optimized for quizzes, interview questions, and progress dashboards.

The backend manages user authentication, quiz logic, scoring algorithms, progress tracking, and database operations with optimized queries for performance.

AI assistance is integrated as a support layer for on-demand explanations without replacing active learning.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Designing a scalable quiz and scoring system

Solution: Modularized quiz logic by subject and difficulty level with efficient database indexing

Challenge: Tracking progress across multiple subjects and question types

Solution: Stored detailed attempt data with normalized schemas for comprehensive analytics

Challenge: Maintaining performance with increasing question database

Solution: Optimized database queries and implemented caching strategies

Challenge: Integrating AI assistance without creating over-dependence

Solution: Positioned AI as a helper for stuck moments, not a shortcut—encouraging independent problem-solving first

Impact & Learning

This project strengthened my understanding of full-stack development using PHP and MySQL in a real-world education workflow.

I gained experience designing quiz engines, building analytics dashboards, implementing user progress tracking, and creating scalable preparation systems that serve actual students preparing for placements.

Future Enhancements
